# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### Do you store prescriptions?

No, we only store data you intentionally add. You can export/delete anytime.

### How does the AI work?

It analyzes your data and regimen, suggests typical dosages and checks interactions. It is not a medical diagnosis.

### Do I need internet access?

Basic reminders work offline; sync and analytics require a connection.

## Privacy & Security

### Is my data secure?

Yes, we use end-to-end encryption and follow GDPR guidelines. Your data is stored locally and never shared without your consent.

### Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your data in PDF or CSV format at any time.

### Can I delete my data?

Absolutely. You can delete your account and all associated data permanently.

## Features

### What medications can I track?

You can track any medication, supplement, or vitamin. The app supports custom dosages and schedules.

### How do reminders work?

Reminders are sent via push notifications at your specified times. You can set multiple reminders per day.

### Can I share data with my doctor?

Yes, you can generate reports and share them with your healthcare provider.

## Pricing

### Is there a free version?

Yes, we offer a free tier with basic features including medication tracking and reminders.

### What's included in the Plus plan?

The Plus plan includes interaction checks, basic analytics, and unlimited medication tracking.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties.

## Support

### How can I get help?

Contact us at support@pillmind.app or use the in-app support feature.

### Do you offer phone support?

Currently, we offer email and in-app support. Phone support may be added in the future.

### What languages do you support?

Currently, we support Ukrainian and English. More languages are coming soon.
